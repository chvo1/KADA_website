About KADA

Authentic Vietnamese coffee, tea, and snacks served with warmth and care in Gilbert, Arizona.

Our Story

KADA Vietnamese Coffee & Tea was born from a love of Vietnamese coffee culture and a desire to share it with our community. From bold Cà Phê Sữa Đá to refreshing fruit teas, every drink is crafted to feel both familiar and exciting.

We aim to create a cozy space where friends can meet, students can study, and families can relax. Whether you are grabbing a quick drink or staying a while, we hope KADA feels like a second home.
